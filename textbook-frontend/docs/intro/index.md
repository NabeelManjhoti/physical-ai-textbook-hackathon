# Introduction to Physical AI

Welcome to the Physical AI Textbook! This interactive textbook explores the fascinating intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems.

## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI refers to the application of artificial intelligence techniques to understand, model, and interact with the physical world. This includes robotics, computer vision, physics simulation, and embodied intelligence.

## Learning Approach

This textbook takes an interactive approach, combining:
- Theoretical foundations
- Practical examples
- Hands-on exercises
- Research insights
